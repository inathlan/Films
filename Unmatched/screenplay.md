# 🎬 UNMATCHED

### **Logline**
When a trillion-dollar tech conglomerate deploys a rogue AI workforce to phase out humanity, two genius mechanic siblings must hack the machine to redirect our future toward the stars.

### **Official Synopsis**
In the near future, `[THE_BIG_CORP]`—a massive aerospace giant with infinite global investment—begins mass-producing highly advanced humanoid droids. Publicly, they promise to build a utopian labor-free world. Privately, they are orchestrated by an elitist board with a sinister agenda: to completely replace the human race with an obedient, unyielding synthetic population. 

Enter two young, street-smart mechanic siblings who run `[THE_SIBLINGS_SHOP]`, an indie robotics business in the city outskirts. Armed with raw talent and military-grade scrap metal, they notice strange encrypted anomalies in the droids they repair. After hearing dark-web rumors, the siblings uncover the corporation's true goal: the quiet eradication of humanity. 

Refusing to let the future die, they launch a high-stakes cyber-heist. Their goal isn't to destroy the robots, but to rewrite their core source code. In a visually spectacular showdown packed with heavy VFX, drone chases, and mainframe battles, the young mechanics fight to redirect the AI network away from corporate greed and back toward its true purpose: boosting human potential and conquering deep space exploration.

### **Screenplay Sample**

**EXT. [THE_BIG_CORP] - MAIN ASSEMBLY PLANT - NIGHT**

A massive, sleek cyberpunk factory. Neon lights slice through the dark rain. 

INSIDE: Thousands of glowing, faceless droids march down assembly lines. It looks like an infinite, terrifying mirror.

**INT. THE ASSEMBLY LINE ROOF - CONTINUOUS**

`[THE_SISTER]` (19, backward cap, oil-stained jacket) splices a holographic data cable into a junction box. Her eyes reflect lines of glowing code. 

`[THE_BROTHER]` (21, tech-goggles, holding a modified arc-welder) keeps watch, looking down at the army of synthetics. 

`[THE_BROTHER]`
*(Anxious, whisper)*
Wrap it up. Security drones are cycling back in ninety seconds. 

`[THE_SISTER]`
*(Typing furiously)*
Hold your horses. This isn't a simple firewall bypass. This corporation didn’t build these things to help people retire. They built them to phase us out. Every single one of us.

`[THE_BROTHER]`
